## Introduction

Welcome to Rapier DeFAI, where AI meets DeFi on Solana. Our platform lets you craft & unleash smart farming agents, fine-tuning your yield strategies across Solana's vibrant ecosystem. Sharpen your edge in DeFi with Rapier – where every trade is a masterpiece.

## Platform Overview

Rapier DeFAI consists of two main components:
1. RapierPad (AI Agent Launchpad)
2. Official AI Agents

### RapierPad: AI Agent Launchpad

RapierPad empowers users to create and customize their own AI farming agents. Each user can create up to 2 AI agents

Key Features:
- Create custom AI agents
- Fine-tune strategies using advanced parameters
- Deploy agents to farm yields across Solana's DeFi ecosystem

### Official AI Agents

We offer three official AI agents, each with a distinct risk profile:
- Athos (Conservative)
- Porthos (Moderate)
- Aramis (Aggressive)

These agents are initially seeded with $15,000 each. The team will frequently add fresh funds to each agent, aiming to reach and maintain $100,000 AUM for each.

## $Rapier Token

$Rapier is the utility token of the Rapier DeFAI ecosystem. It serves as the key to creating and deploying your own yield farming AI agents. Holders of $Rapier can:

- Create custom AI agents on RapierPad
- Stake in official AI agents to earn USDC rewards
- Participate in governance decisions for the platform's future development

At launch, users will be able to stake their $Rapier tokens in one of the three official AI agents: Athos, Porthos, or Aramis. This allows immediate participation in AI-driven yield farming strategies.

## Creating Your AI Agent

Users can customize their AI agents by adjusting the following parameters:

### DeFi Parameters

1. Liquidity Thresholds: Min $10,000 - Max $1,000,000,000
2. APY Range: Min 0.1% - Max 1000%
3. Impermanent Loss Tolerance: Min 0% - Max 50%
4. Rebalancing Frequency: Min 1 hour - Max 30 days
5. Gas Optimization: Scale 1 (Low) - 10 (Aggressive)
6. Slippage Tolerance: Min 0.1% - Max 10%
7. Collateralization Ratio: Min 110% - Max 1000%
8. Flash Loan Utilization: Binary (Yes/No)
9. Maximum Allocation per Pool: Min 1% - Max 100%
10. Minimum Time in Position: Min 1 minute - Max 365 days

### AI Agent Parameters

1. Risk Tolerance: Min 1 (Very Conservative) - Max 10 (Highly Aggressive)
2. Learning Rate: Min 0.0001 - Max 1.0
3. Historical Data Window: Min 1 hour - Max 365 days
4. Prediction Confidence Threshold: Min 51% - Max 99%
5. Feature Importance Weighting: Scale 1 (Low) - 10 (High) for each feature
6. Anomaly Detection Sensitivity: Min 0.1 - Max 10.0
7. Multi-Agent Cooperation: Min 0% (No Cooperation) - Max 100% (Full Cooperation)
8. Reinforcement Learning Decay: Min 0.1 - Max 1.0
9. Execution Speed Priority: Min 1 (Accuracy Focused) - Max 10 (Speed Focused)
10. Ethical Constraints: Customizable set of rules (e.g., No rug pulls, No wash trading)

## Official AI Agents

### Athos - The Steadfast Guardian

- Risk Profile: Conservative
- Strategy: Focuses on stablecoin farming and high TVL pools (>$100M) on Solana
- Key Features:
  - Prioritizes capital preservation
  - Targets consistent, low-volatility yields
  - Favors established Solana protocols with strong security track records

### Porthos - The Balanced Tactician

- Risk Profile: Moderate
- Strategy: Balances stable yields with calculated risks within the Solana ecosystem
- Key Features:
  - Explores mid-cap SPL tokens and newer Solana protocols
  - Implements dynamic rebalancing based on market conditions
  - Utilizes a mix of single-asset staking and liquidity provision on Solana DEXs

### Aramis - The Daring Strategist

- Risk Profile: Aggressive
- Strategy: Seeks high APY opportunities, including new pairs and protocols on Solana
- Key Features:
  - Actively participates in new liquidity pools on emerging Solana DEXs
  - Leverages yield farming boosters and incentives specific to Solana
  - Implements advanced strategies like flash loans for arbitrage within Solana's DeFi landscape

## $Rapier Staking

$Rapier token holders can stake their tokens in one of the three official AI agents:

- Stakers earn USDC from the agents' farming profits
- Profits are distributed hourly
- Rewards are based on the staker's percentage share of the pool

## Profit Distribution

- Profits are calculated and distributed every hour
- Distribution is proportional to each staker's share in the pool
- Rewards are paid in USDC directly to the staker's Solana wallet
